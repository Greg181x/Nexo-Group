About NEXO Group

Nexo Group is a future-focused initiative designed to build, connect, and scale talent, teams, and ideas across multiple industries.
Current pillars include Esports and Creative, with room for future divisions.

About Us

Nexo Esports is a German-based esports organization founded in 2025 as part of the broader Nexo Group
ecosystem. Built with a forward-thinking vision, Nexo Esports focuses on developing competitive talent,
fostering strong teams, and creating innovative opportunities within the esports industry.

As a core pillar of Nexo Group, the organization is driven by a mission to build, connect, and scale talent,
teams, and ideas across multiple industries. Alongside Esports, Nexo Group currently operates within the
Creative sector, with the flexibility to expand into additional divisions in the future.

Rooted in Germany and shaped by a global mindset, Nexo Esports aims to become a sustainable and
influential presence in modern esports by combining competition, creativity, and long-term growth.
